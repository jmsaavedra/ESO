
electromechanical solenoid orchestra
>benjamin bacon
>joe saavedra

exhibited as part of the weather tunnel during TransLife new media exhibition at the national art museum in china, july and august 2011. Also exhibited during Beijing Design Week, 2011. 

maxMSP patches connect to Pachube, pull 8 data streams from 6 different feeds (48 streams total). Data is used to compose generative music, which is sent out via MIDI to solenoid cages that "perform the data" in real-time.
